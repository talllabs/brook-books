BROOK'S BOOK ~ CHAPTER 2

Sam is a small rat.

Sam sat on the mat in the sun.

The sun was hot on the mat.

Sam ran off the mat.

Sam ran to a log.

The log was cool and dark.

Sam sat by the log and rested.

A bug ran past Sam.

Sam watched the bug run.

Sam ran back to the mat.

The sun was low in the sky.

Sam sat on the mat and felt calm.
